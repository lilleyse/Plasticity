Ian Lilley and Sean Lilley

Plasticity

Build steps:

Go to tools/project_builder and double click the build_win.bat batch script. 
Now go back to the build folder and open the solution file.

Computer requirements:

Windows, with Visual Studio
OpenGL 4 (mainly due to tessellation)

Controls:

Use mouse drag to rotate around scene. 
Middle mouse click and drag to pan. 
Right click to throw projectiles.
'W' to turn on wireframe
'T' to enable tessellation
